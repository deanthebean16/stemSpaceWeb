    Workshop Schedule

    We are hosting 4 FREE workshop sessions every Saturday of July 2026 from 11AM - 1PM at 7030 Woodbine Ave #801, Markham, ON L3R 6G2. Snacks & drinks provided, as well as something for participants to take home!

    Session 1 Jul 4 - 2026

    STEM: Space Junkbots 🤖: Recycle to the Moon!

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 14 / Grades 3 - 8)

    11:00 - 11:45 AM

    Turn everyday recycled materials into a moving robot powered by a real motor! Participants will explore circuits and engineering as they design, build, and test their own Junkbots before taking on a fun "Race to the Moon" challenge. This hands-on workshop encourages creativity, innovation, and sustainability while showing how engineers use technology and problem-solving to tackle real-world challenges.

    Mental Wellness: 📝 Bullet Journal Design Studio

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 14 / Grades 3 - 8)

    12:00 - 12:45 PM

    Transform a blank notebook into a personalized tool for creativity, organization, and growth. Participants will design custom pages to track goals, habits, moods, and daily activities while exploring how planners and data tracking can help us stay focused and balanced. By the end of the workshop, each participant will leave with a one-of-a-kind journal that reflects their personality and supports their everyday success.

    Session 2 Jul 11 - 2026

    STEM: 🎈🏁 Balloon Racers Engineering Challenge

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 11 / Grades 3 - 6)

    11:00 - 11:45 AM

    Put your engineering skills to the test in this exciting design challenge! Working in teams, participants will build a balloon-powered car and compete to see whose design travels the farthest and fastest. Along the way, they'll learn about Newton's Laws of Motion, the engineering design process, and how creativity, teamwork, and perseverance help solve real-world problems.

    Mental Wellness: 🧪 Squishy Science: Design Your Stress-Away

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 11 / Grades 3 - 6)

    12:00 - 12:45 PM

    Turn simple materials into a fun, personalized stress toy! Participants will design, build, and decorate their own squishies while exploring how different materials respond to pressure and movement. Through creativity, product design, and hands-on experimentation, youth will create a unique sensory tool that supports focus, relaxation, and self-expression—plus a custom squishy to take home and enjoy.

    Session 3 Jul 18 - 2026

    STEM: Lava Lamp Science: 🫧 Bubbles & Density

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 10 / Grades 3 - 5)

    11:00 - 11:45 AM

    Create your own colourful lava lamp while discovering the science behind its creation! Participants will explore concepts like density and chemical reactions as they mix ingredients, do fun experiments, and observe the bubbling "lava" effect. This hands-on workshop combines STEM learning with creativity, and everyone takes home their own lava lamp to continue experimenting with.

    Mental Wellness: 🪨 Clay Creations: Worry Stones

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 10 / Grades 3 - 5)

    12:00 - 12:45 PM

    Get creative and bring your ideas to life with air-dry clay! Participants will sculpt worry stones, mini creations, or fidget-friendly designs while learning about materials, shapes, and design. This relaxing, hands-on workshop encourages creativity, mindfulness, and self-expression—and everyone gets to take home their own unique creation.

    Session 4 Jul 25 - 2026

    STEM: Mini Greenhouses 🏡: Build & Grow Family Workshop

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 14 / Grades 3 - 8)

    11:00 - 11:45 AM

    Family workshop! Build and take home your own mini greenhouse while learning how plants grow! Families will plant seeds and explore concepts like plant biology and how greenhouses create the perfect environment for growth. A fun, hands-on activity that combines STEM learning with gardening and nature.

    Mental Wellness: Paint & Plant 🎨: Creative Gardens

    (Recommended Ages 8 - 14 / Grades 3 - 8)

    12:00 - 12:45 PM

    Design a colourful flower pot, then bring it to life with your own seeds to grow at home! Participants will explore creativity, colour, and plant science while decorating their pots and learning what plants need to thrive. This fun, hands-on workshop combines art, nature, and STEM, leaving everyone with a personalized creation they can nurture long after the workshop ends.
