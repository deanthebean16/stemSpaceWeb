    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions – Common problems and how to fix

    Is it really free?

    Yes! All our workshops, snacks, and take-home kits are completely free!

    Can siblings attend?

    Yes, as long as they each get signed up individually on the google form.

    Do parents stay?

    No, most of our workshops are kids only. This is to give them an opportunity to learn and connect with their peers! However, we do have a family greenhouse building session, where parents are welcome to come with their children!

    What should children bring to the workshops?

    Children should come in comfortable clothing and closed toed shoes ideally. A water bottle is also recommended!

    What if we can't attend?

    Please email stemspace.collective@gmail.com 48 in advance to the workshop date.

    Is food provided?

    Yes, snacks and drinks will be provided.

    What ages are appropriate?

    Each session has a recommended age (typically grades 2-8), but we welcome youth of all ages to attend!
