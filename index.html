We are a student-run organization funded by the Canada Service Corps. This summer, we are excited to offer free STEM and Mental Health workshops for youth to explore STEM through hands-on activities while learning strategies to manage stress, build resilience, and support their well-being.

We are hosting four workshop sessions every Saturday in July from 11AM - 1PM. We look forward to learning & growing with you this summer!

Our Mission

We value creating a supportive space where youth can explore STEM through fun, hands-on experiences while prioritizing mental wellness and balance. We aim to inspire creativity, confidence, and curiosity, by combining learning with engaging de-stressing activities that help youth feel connected, empowered, and motivated.
